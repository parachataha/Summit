About Summit

Our Values

Here at Summit, we believe anyone should be able to have basic mathematics knowledge. With that, Summit is accessible online and offline* for anyone. Working on any* device. Summit allows people from any origins, any nationalities and any race to learn for free, with an unlimited supply of mathematics content. Click the buttons below to find out more.

Find out more Start Learning

Our Goals

Our goal is to make Summit a worldwide accessible platform that will allow anyone to learn maths. Maths is an essential subject that everyone should have the right to learn, however not everyone can. Summit's goal is to try to destroy that barrier and let anyone, at anytime to learn mathmetics and their own pace.
